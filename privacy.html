Privacy Policy

Self-Discipline Brain

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Self-Discipline Brain handles user data in order to provide screen time management and digital wellbeing features.

Data Collection

- The app uses Apple’s FamilyControls API to manage screen time and restrict app usage.
- We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary for screen time management.
- All data is stored locally on the user’s device whenever possible.

Data Sharing

- No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
- FamilyControls data is used strictly for providing app restriction and wellbeing features.

User Rights

Users remain in full control of their data. You may revoke permissions or delete the app at any time, which removes all associated data from your device.
