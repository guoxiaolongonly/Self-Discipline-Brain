Self-Discipline Brain

A digital wellbeing app powered by Apple's FamilyControls

About

Self-Discipline Brain helps users reduce dependency on entertainment apps and improve focus. By setting daily goals and switching between modes such as Work, Study, and Family Time, users can build healthier digital habits.

Why FamilyControls

The app relies on Apple's FamilyControls API to enforce app usage limits and provide screen time insights. These features are essential to the core functionality of promoting digital wellbeing.

Key Features

  • Set daily goals for healthy device usage
  • Custom modes: Work, Study, Family Time
  • Limit the time allowed for selected apps
  • Positive reminders highlighting brain health benefits

Privacy Commitment

  • Data is stored on-device whenever possible
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • FamilyControls is used strictly for screen time management
